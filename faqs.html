FAQs

  • I filled out the order form, but what's next?

    We are a small startup company. As such, we will call you to verify the details of your order, take payment and provide shipping dates.

  • How long does shipping usually take?

    We ship using standard carriers like USPS, FedEx and UPS. As such, all orders are shipped within 2 days of order and arrive within 5 days.

  • How can I track my order?

    Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your package.

  • Can I change or cancel my order?

    You can change or cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it. Please contact our customer service team for assistance.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept Zelle and Venmo.
